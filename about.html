✔️ What D2LA is

D2LA (Destiny 2 Loadout Advisor) is a curated PvE loadout guide designed to help Guardians quickly find effective builds for raids and dungeons. Whether you're a returning player or a seasoned fireteam leader. Loadouts are organized by activity and class, with recommendations for both standard and alternative gear.

❌ What D2LA is not

D2LA isn’t a build simulator, a stat calculator, or a replacement for your favourite Destiny content creators. It doesn’t track your inventory or loadouts. While the loadouts have been curated via community concensus, it still remains an opinion and thus should be taken as so. This tool is merely a fast, focused assistant to help you get raid-ready. In the end, just roll with whatever loadout gets you the clear.

🗨️ Things to note

Some encounters, whether in raids or dungeons, have been intentionally omitted. These are typically traversal zones or ad-heavy platforming segments with minimal combat mechanics. Recommendations for these areas are left to *Guardian preference*.
