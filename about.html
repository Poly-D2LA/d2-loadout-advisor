What is D2LA?

D2LA (Destiny 2 Loadout Advisor) is a curated PvE loadout guide designed to help Guardians quickly find effective builds for raids and dungeons. Whether you're a returning player or a seasoned fireteam leader. Loadouts are organized by activity and class, with recommendations for both standard and alternative gear.

What D2LA isn’t.

D2LA isn’t a build simulator, a stat calculator, or a replacement for your favourite Destiny content creators. It doesn’t track your inventory or loadouts. While the loadouts have been curated via community concensus, it still remains an opinion and thus should be taken as so. It’s a fast, focused tool to help you get raid-ready without scrolling through spreadsheets or watching 30-minute videos. This tool is merely an assistant but as always, run whatever loadout you want to run. If it helps you get the clear, great!
